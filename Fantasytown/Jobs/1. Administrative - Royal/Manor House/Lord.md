The highest title in town, makes the final decision in most aspects and protected by guards at all time. 

Basic to do list:
--
Priorities and tasks vary with personality.

 - Talk with high value members of the keep
 - open court and listen to citizens
 - eat at great table
 - oversee the economy and laws
 - be the final judge in cases and decisions
 - lazy around
 - wander around town
 - invite other lords to represent town
 - invite foreigners into town
 - expedition into another town
 - rally the guard in cases of emergency

Personalities of lords:
--
In the case of lords and other professions, their personality will be added as a profix after their name, example: Greg the kind.

Lords can have multiple personalities, their most prevalent one will be the prefix.

- Kind
    - more forgiving when resolving cases
    - might give less tax 
    - might offer money around when wandering
    - higher pay for everyone in town
    - more reputation in town
- Greedy
	- Less pay to people in town
	- More external income to treasury
	- More taxes
	- Higher reputation with other lords
	- more accepting to bribes
- Lazy
	- will spend more time doing the lazy around task
	- might skip meetings and not go outside
	- less reputation with town and other lords
	- less likely to participate in cases
	- less likely to organize guard
- Veteran
	- better at organizing guard
	- more reputation with other lords
	- much better at fighting
	- more likely to organize guard
	- more volatile
- Reckless
	- Extremely likely to organize guard
	- will often give wrong accusations
	- more gullible 
	- more likely to cause attacks from other towns
